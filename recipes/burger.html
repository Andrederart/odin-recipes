BEST BURGER IN TOWN

Hello a brief introduction to the burger of all burgers.

This burger contains meat.
It's created through sustainable means and offers great taste.
Here's some sought after information regarding said burger.

Ingrediants

  • Brioche buns
  • 1 pound of ground meat
  • cheese of any type
  • lettuce tomatoes bacon
  • sauce of your choice

Directions

  1. toast the brioche buns in the toaster for 1 minute
  2. simultaneously cook the ground meat and salt it to preference
  3. once meat is cooked add cheese on top for 30 seconds and continue cooking
  4. at this point spread sauce on the bottom bun,
    place the cheese burger on top and garnish with lettuce tomatos bacon.
  5. don't forget to bookmark and come back!!!!!!!!!!
